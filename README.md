## Passive Until Provoked (PUP)

**Hostile mobs don’t start the fight. You do.**

Passive Until Provoked redefines Minecraft’s threat model. No more constant harassment while exploring. Hostile mobs ignore you by default and only become aggressive when provoked. Combat becomes intentional, not unavoidable.

---

## Core Features

* Hostile mobs ignore players until attacked
* Instant retaliation with full vanilla behavior
* No AI removal or invasive rewrites
* Works with most modded hostile mobs
* Lightweight, event-driven design
* Clean, configurable behavior

---

## Behavior Highlights

* Zombies will not chase you unless provoked
* Skeletons will not shoot unless attacked
* Creepers will not swell near players unless provoked
* Endermen retain vanilla behavior by default
* Boss mobs remain unaffected

---

## Why Use This Mod

* Explore caves and structures without constant interruption
* Shift gameplay toward strategy and control
* Perfect for tech, exploration, and immersion modpacks
* Keeps vanilla combat intensity when it matters

---

## Compatibility

* Built for NeoForge 1.20.1
* Designed for high compatibility with modded environments
* Applies broadly to hostile mob behavior, not hardcoded lists

---

## Installation

1. Install NeoForge 1.20.1
2. Place the mod `.jar` into your `mods` folder
3. Launch Minecraft

---

## Expected Behavior

* Hostile mobs ignore players until attacked
* Once provoked, mobs behave exactly like vanilla
* No global AI breakage or unintended side effects
